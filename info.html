Bhumi Studio is a Paris and New York–based creative agency whose work moves between fashion editorial, art direction, and brand identity. The studio's practice is rooted in observation and restraint, capturing quiet moments of beauty with clarity and intention. Founded with a background in fine art and visual communication, Bhumi carries a sense of rhythm and lyricism—both personal and precise.

Now operating across continents, the studio approaches each project with a quiet curiosity, favoring natural light, fleeting gestures, and unscripted compositions. Bhumi's work reflects a sustained engagement with presence: an attempt to look closely, and with care.

The studio's projects explore themes of solitude, connection, memory, and place—shaped by the experience of working between cultures. Across personal and commissioned work, Bhumi maintains a consistent approach: imagery that feels lived-in and emotionally true, yet formally considered.

Bhumi's practice increasingly extends into film, spatial design, and longer-form narratives, returning to questions of home, identity, and the act of seeing.
